Things to do

There are lots of things to do in Seattle. These are some of our favorites!

Pike Place

Nifty market downtown. Avoid the Starbucks line.

Restaraunts

Plenty of Clouds and Commmunion are two of our favorite restaraunts in the city. We definitely recommend stopping by if you get the chance.

Farmer's Markets

Seattle has some wonderful summer fruits! Grab a pint of berries (or six).

Volunteer Park

They got some ducks and Bruce Lee's grave.

Mariners Game

Go Mariners!

Seafair

It's loud, but hey, if you like planes, they're there!
