Our Story

2017

How We Met

We met after our first year of college, interning for a CS professor. Noah showed up late (he claims it's not his fault), and Zené was unimpressed. After spending the summer not talking to each other, we *finally* connected in the last week of our internship. This is the first picture we took together on the last day.

2017

First Date

For our first date, we had brunch at Daley's, a diner near campus. Once we were officially together, we made it a tradition to go to Daley's at the start of every quarter.

2020

Moving to Seattle

In 2020, mid-pandemic, we graduated and moved to Seattle to start work as software engineers.

2023

Juniper Joins the Family

In January, we adopted our beautiful cat daughter Juniper. Look at that perfect angel!

2023

Becoming a Teacher

In 2023, Zené escaped tech and started grad school to become a teacher! We celebrated with a trip to London and Amsterdam.

2024

The Proposal

On July 13, 2024, Zené proposed to Noah with a beautifully crafted crossword puzzle. It was so well done in fact, that Noah didn't realize he was being proposed to. He finished eating the ring pop before all the pictures could be taken...

2026

Now

We are getting married!!!!!
